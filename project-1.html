Project Overview

"IssueConnect" is a cutting-edge web-based Complaint Management System meticulously designed to cater to the specific requirements of NIT Patna. The project is dedicated to offering an intuitive and efficient platform for both students and staff to seamlessly report and manage complaints. It incorporates tailored modules for students, administrators, and staff, ensuring a personalized experience for each user role.

The platform's primary focus is to empower students with the ability to effortlessly file complaints, track their progress, and receive real-time updates on their submissions. On the administrator side, a comprehensive dashboard provides oversight of all complaints, enables task assignments to staff members, facilitates progress monitoring, and ensures timely resolution. Staff members have access to a dedicated interface that enables them to efficiently manage their assigned tasks.

Utilizing advanced technologies such as Next.js, Node.js, and MongoDB, "IssueConnect" guarantees a robust and scalable system architecture. Integration of email notifications further enhances communication and transparency, contributing to the efficiency of the complaint resolution process. With a focus on modern design, responsiveness, and user-friendly interfaces, the project aims to deliver an engaging and effective solution.

Beyond its technical prowess, "IssueConnect" promotes a sense of community and collaboration within the institution. By facilitating prompt complaint resolution and offering a transparent process, the platform contributes to an enhanced campus experience for students and staff, aligning with NIT Patna's commitment to excellence and student well-being.
